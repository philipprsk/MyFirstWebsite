About the Project

Welcome to my passion project - recreating our community's most cherished landmarks using LEGO bricks. Each build is a labor of love, carefully researched and constructed to capture both the architectural details and the emotional significance of these special places.

What started as a simple hobby has evolved into a comprehensive documentation of our local heritage. Through the medium of LEGO, I aim to celebrate the buildings that define our community's character and preserve their memory for future generations.
